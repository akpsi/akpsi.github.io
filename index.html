Alpha Kappa Psi at Oregon State University

In 1904, Alpha Kappa Psi was founded on the values of Brotherhood, Knowledge, Integrity, Service and Unity which the Theta Chapter upholds with the upmost dignity. Alpha Kappa Psi has grown to be the oldest and largest business fraternity in the world! As members in a premier professional business fraternity we cultivate and maintain relationships with our current brothers and our alumni network as well. As one of the best professional co-ed business fraternities we are passionate hardworking group of young professionals that push each other to succeed.

Shaping people, shaping business!

The Theta Chapter of Alpha Kappa Psi is well-known for its outstanding integration of both Brotherhood and Professional Development. We strive for a diversity of eager students and welcome ALL majors and years* to apply for membership. Members have the advantage to not only enhance their professional skills through workshops, site tours, and guest speakers, but they also get the opportunity to create long lasting friendships with peers who are motivated and driven to succeed.

What We Do

Service

We have been partnered with our local Heartland Humane Society for many years. Our members believe in giving back to the community so we do our best to participate in volunteer work when we can as a chapter. Our previous service work include Corvallis Marathons, Relay For Life, and Boys & Girls Club of Corvallis.

Social

We are a professional fraternity, as much as we promote professionalism within our chapter, we also believe in the brotherhood aspect of the fraternity. Our members attend retreats and events on & off campus to network and improve their social communication skills so they can be better prepared as business leaders.

Professional

Our group of professionals coordinate professional events for the benefit of the entire chapter. These include our brand new investment & business seminars, corporate tours, and guest speakers through the year. As the premier developer of business leaders, our chapter has exclusively accepted to partner with non-business clubs at Oregon State University to consult in matters of logistics, marketing, and finances.

Conferences

We currently attend two conferences as a chapter. The first being our annual conference in the Northwest Pacific Region, PBLI. Our second conference is our bi-annual Convention where we vote on our international constitutions as a chapter and elect the next executive members to run the fraternity at the highest level. We also have a few conferences that are offered to members which members can apply to throughout the year.
